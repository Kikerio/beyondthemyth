Project Introduction

Created to celebrate NASA's 70th anniversary, this archive explores the shifting paradigms—the myths, models, and measurements—that have defined humanity's understanding of the cosmos. By tracing the evolution of these ideas through detailed timelines, we reveal how the continuous advancement of scientific tools, observational instruments, and pure mathematics has allowed us to make groundbreaking discoveries and correct past misconceptions. Beyond the Myth navigates this profound journey, from the earliest sky-gazing of ancient priests to the frontiers of quantum physics, uncovering the truths hidden behind our oldest celestial stories.
